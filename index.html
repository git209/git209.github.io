Research Vision

From microscale engineering to real-world impact

Our research focuses on intelligent microsystems that connect fundamental engineering with translational applications. We develop enabling technologies for clinical monitoring, point-of-care diagnostics, programmable microfluidics, adaptive robotic systems, and next-generation thermal management.

Research Areas

Interdisciplinary platforms and applications

Our work combines device design, materials, fluid mechanics, sensing, machine learning, and system integration.

01

Digital Microfluidics

Electrowetting-based systems for programmable droplet generation, transport, mixing, and biomedical processing.

02

AI-Assisted Healthcare

Optoelectronic sensing and data-driven prediction for real-time monitoring and personalized clinical decision support.

03

Biomedical Microfluidics

Microfluidic and acoustofluidic technologies for filtration, diagnostics, sample preparation, and point-of-care testing.

04

Wearable Sensors

Flexible, paper-based, and integrated sensors for physiological monitoring, human–machine interfaces, and digital health.

05

Soft Robotics

Programmable magnetic materials and compliant robotic systems for adaptive motion and biomedical applications.

06

Thermal & Energy Systems

Thermal-fluid design and intelligent cooling strategies for batteries, electronics, and high-power systems.
